CFTG-EDC

Prepare your Telegram Bot, then deploy to Cloudflare in one click.

1
Create Telegram Bot

Open @BotFather on Telegram, send /newbot, follow the prompts, then paste the token below.

2
Activate Your Bot

Open your newly created bot in Telegram (tap the link @BotFather gave you), and send /start. This allows the bot to send you messages.

3
Get Your Chat ID

Open @userinfobot on Telegram, send /start, it will reply with your ID. Paste it below.

A
Open Deploy Page

Open the Cloudflare deploy page in a new tab. First time? You'll need to connect your GitHub account — just click "New GitHub Connection" and authorize. Keep this page open to copy values later.

B
Copy TG_BOT_TOKEN

Click to copy, then switch to the deploy page and paste it in the TG_BOT_TOKEN field.

C
Copy TG_CHAT_ID

Click to copy, then switch to the deploy page and paste it in the TG_CHAT_ID field.
